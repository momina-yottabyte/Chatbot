## 1. PURPOSE
YOTTABYTE ("the Company") is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of personal information and ensuring compliance with Data Protection principles outlined in this policy.

---

## 2. SCOPE
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who handle personal information on behalf of YOTTABYTE. It covers all personal information collected, processed, stored, or transmitted by the Company, whether in electronic or physical form.

---

## 3. DATA PROTECTION PRINCIPLES

The Company adheres to the following data protection principles:

### 3.1 Lawfulness, Fairness, and Transparency
- Personal data is processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently.
- Individuals are informed about purposes and legal basis.
- Consent or lawful basis is ensured.

### 3.2 Purpose Limitation
- Data is collected only for specific and legitimate purposes.
- No incompatible processing unless legally required.

### 3.3 Data Minimization
- Only necessary and relevant data is collected.
- Data is not retained longer than needed.  
- Refer to Asset Management Policy and Operational Security Policy.

### 3.4 Accuracy
- Data is kept accurate and up to date.
- Individuals can request corrections.

### 3.5 Storage Limitation
- Data is retained only as long as necessary.
- Secure disposal is ensured.

### 3.6 Integrity and Confidentiality
- Security measures protect against unauthorized access.
- Includes encryption, access controls, and monitoring.

### 3.7 Accountability
- Documentation of policies, controls, and processes is maintained.
- Regular reviews ensure continuous improvement.

---

## 4. RESPONSIBILITIES

### 4.1 Management
- Ensure implementation of data protection measures.
- Provide resources and training.
- Maintain privacy governance.

### 4.2 Employees
- Follow data protection practices.
- Handle data securely.
- Report breaches or concerns.
- Participate in training.

---

## 5. CONSENT COLLECTION

### 5.1 Consent Requirements
- Must be obtained before processing data.
- Must be free, informed, and explicit.
- Must clearly state purpose.
- Can be withdrawn anytime.

### 5.2 Consent Collection Process
- Clearly explain purpose.
- Use simple and understandable language.
- Use active opt-in (e.g., checkboxes).
- Provide granular consent options.
- Maintain consent records.
- Allow easy withdrawal.
- Regularly review process.

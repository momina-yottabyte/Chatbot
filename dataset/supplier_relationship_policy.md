# 1. PURPOSE

YOTTABYTE must ensure that contracted third parties apply equally stringent controls in managing and protecting YOTTABYTE’s sensitive data. Adequate contracts and due diligence processes must be in place.

---

# 2. SCOPE

This policy defines rules for managing security over third parties (suppliers, vendors, etc.) who have direct or indirect access to YOTTABYTE IT systems and data.

Applies to:
- Corporations  
- Individuals  
- Sub-contractors  
- External service providers  

---

# 3. POLICY STATEMENT(S)

- Third parties must contractually and operationally commit to YOTTABYTE’s security and compliance obligations.

### Mandatory Requirements in Agreements:

- Non-disclosure agreements (NDA)
- Notification of security incidents
- Protection of confidentiality, integrity, and availability
- Contract termination clauses for non-compliance
- Subcontractor compliance obligations

---

## Supplier Security Requirements

- Suppliers must agree to YOTTABYTE security requirements
- Access types must be defined, monitored, and controlled
- Security obligations must be documented

### Security Agreements Must Include:

- Identification of risks from third-party access
- Legal and regulatory requirements:
  - Data protection
  - Data residency
  - Intellectual property rights

---

## 3.1 Selection and Security Evaluation of Suppliers

- Suppliers handling Confidential/Restricted data must undergo risk assessment
- Risk-based security criteria must be established
- HR/Procurement conducts assessments

### Acceptable Security Validation Methods:

- Self-assessment (ISO 27001 or equivalent)
- Certification (ISO 27001)
- External audit reports

### Additional Requirements:

- Maintain third-party inventory with risk classification (High/Medium/Low)
- Documented risk acceptance required for non-compliant suppliers
- Suppliers must provide documentation on:
  - Risk management
  - Staffing
  - Security processes

---

## 3.2 Supplier Service Delivery Management

- Monitor supplier performance against business and security requirements
- High-risk suppliers require **annual security review**

### Review Process:

- Conducted by YOTTABYTE security team
- Based on ISMS policies and standards
- Results shared with supplier
- Supplier must commit to remediation timelines

### Change Management:

- All changes must be:
  - Documented
  - Aligned with business processes
  - Reassessed for risk if required

---

# 4. ANNUAL REVIEW

- ISMS documents reviewed annually or upon updates
- Internal audits conducted at least once per year
- Reports submitted to Information Security Forum (ISF)
- Continuous improvement ensured

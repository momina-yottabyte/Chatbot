## 1. PURPOSE

The purpose of this policy is to establish guidelines and procedures for maintaining a secure information environment while employees work remotely. YOTTABYTE recognizes the importance of safeguarding sensitive data and protecting company resources, even outside the traditional office setting.

This policy ensures the **confidentiality, integrity, and availability** of company information while enabling employees to work efficiently and securely from remote locations.

---

## 2. SCOPE

This policy applies to all systems, people, and processes that constitute the YOTTABYTE information systems, including all employees and third parties who have access to company systems.

---

## 3. POLICY STATEMENTS

### 3.1 Work Location

- The approved primary remote work location is the employee’s home address on file with HR.
- Any change in location must be approved by the employee’s manager.
- Public places (e.g., coffee shops, restaurants, airports) are **not suitable** for handling confidential information.
- Employees must be cautious when using public Wi-Fi due to security risks.
- Company devices must:
  - Be used **only by the employee**
  - Not be shared with family or others  

- Remote access must use:
  - Authorized VPN connections  
  - Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)  

---

### 3.2 Device Security

- Only approved company tools may be used for work.
- Software installation on company devices requires approval.
- Company equipment must:
  - Be used only for business purposes  
  - Remain in secure environments  
  - Be accessed only by authorized personnel  

---

### 3.3 Security and Compliance

- Employees must follow all information security policies and data protection regulations.
- Employees are responsible for:
  - Protecting company data and assets  
  - Reporting theft or loss immediately to HR  

- Regular backups must be maintained.
- IT will provide guidance for data recovery.

---

### 3.4 Business Continuity

- Employees must inform their manager and HR promptly in case of:
  - Internet outages  
  - Power failures  
  - Any issue affecting work  

---

## 4. GENERAL GUIDELINES

When working remotely, employees must ensure:

- Availability during working hours for communication
- A quiet, distraction-free work environment
- Adequate lighting
- Ergonomic setup with proper back support  
  *(Note: Company does not provide furniture for remote setups)*

---

## 5. ANNUAL REVIEW

- ISMS documents must be reviewed annually or upon updates.
- Internal audits must be conducted at least once per year.
- Reports must be submitted to the **Information Security Forum (ISF)**.
- All processes must be reviewed for compliance and improvement.

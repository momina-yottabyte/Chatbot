## 1. PURPOSE

The purpose of this policy is to ensure that all incidents are properly detected, managed, monitored, and communicated. Every employee is responsible for understanding and following these guidelines.

---

## 2. SCOPE

This policy applies to all systems, people, and processes within YOTTABYTE, including employees and third parties.

---

## 3. POLICY STATEMENTS

### 3.1 Information Management Process

- Incident Management Team (IMT) is established to manage incidents  
- IMT is formed by Information Security Forum (ISF)  
- IMT availability must be 24/7  
- IMT list is reviewed quarterly  
- Incidents are reported via: **secops@yottabyte.ltd**  
- IMT Lead logs incidents in **JIRA** and assigns ticket  
- IMT Lead assigns incident to team member  
- Root cause and impact analysis is performed  
- Evidence is collected and corrective action taken  
- Legal advisor is involved if required  
- Quarterly review of incidents is conducted  

---

### 3.2 Guidelines for Incident Management

- Legal coordination:
  - Verify evidence usability in court  
  - Decide reporting to authorities  

- Incident handling rules:
  - Do NOT test suspected weaknesses  
  - Immediately report incidents  

- IMT objective:
  - Restore services quickly  
  - Minimize business impact  

- Reporting:
  - Use Incident Reporting Form (Annex A)  
  - Email to IMT + Manager  

---

### 3.3 Responsibilities of Incident Management Team (IMT)

IMT must collect:

- **Basic Information:**
  - Reporter name/email  
  - Department  
  - Date/time  
  - Contact number  

- **Incident Tracking:**
  - Ticket number (JIRA)  
  - Incident classification  
  - Priority level  

#### Priority Levels

- **Critical:** Full business stop → Immediate recovery  
- **High:** Partial disruption → Immediate recovery  
- **Medium:** Business continues → Not urgent  
- **Low:** Minor issue  

#### Additional Details

- Nature of problem  
- Root cause  
- Action taken  
- Closeout authority  
- Verification authority  

---

### Evidence Handling

- **Paper Evidence:**
  - Secure storage  
  - Record finder, location, time  
  - No tampering  

- **Digital Evidence:**
  - Create copies/mirror images  
  - Maintain logs  
  - Secure original data  

---

### 3.4 Goals of Incident Management Team

- Restore service quickly  
- Minimize disruption  
- Manage full incident lifecycle  
- Support operations  

#### Quarterly Meeting Agenda

- Policy review  
- Resolution time analysis  
- Improvements  
- Training needs  
- Preventive checklist updates  

---

## 4. ANNUAL REVIEW

- Annual ISMS document review  
- Annual internal audit  
- Report submitted to ISF  
- Continuous compliance monitoring

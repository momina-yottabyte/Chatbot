# Acceptable Use Policy

## Overview

This policy defines the acceptable use of all technology and information assets belonging to YOTTABYTE. It ensures protection against risks such as reputational damage, legal issues, compliance violations, operational disruptions, and financial loss.

All employees, contractors, and third parties must follow these guidelines.

---

## Purpose

This policy establishes acceptable use requirements for all technology assets managed by YOTTABYTE, including third-party managed services.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to:

- Employees
- Representatives
- Contractors
- Consultants
- Suppliers

It covers all information and technology assets owned or managed by YOTTABYTE.

---

## General Principles

Users must follow these principles:

1. **Responsibility**  
   Users are responsible for protecting data, systems, networks, and applications.

2. **Compliance**  
   Users must follow all laws, regulations, and contractual obligations.

3. **Acceptable Use**  
   Resources must be used only for business purposes.

4. **Security**  
   Users must protect assets from unauthorized access, damage, or theft.

5. **Reporting**  
   Security incidents must be reported immediately to the Incident Management Team (IMT).

---

## Acceptable Use

Allowed uses include:

- Performing company business activities
- Communicating with customers, suppliers, and partners
- Accessing company systems as per policies
- Any use approved by management

---

## Unacceptable Use

The following are strictly prohibited:

- Accessing or sharing illegal or inappropriate content
- Hacking, malware, or disrupting systems
- Using company resources for personal profit
- Sharing confidential data without authorization
- Harassment, discrimination, or threats
- Violating laws or regulations

---

## User Responsibilities

Users must:

- Follow all information security policies and guidelines
- Be accountable for actions performed using their accounts
- Complete required security and privacy training annually
- Open files only from trusted sources
- Protect assigned devices and report loss/theft
- Use strong and secure passwords
- Lock systems when unattended
- Access systems remotely only through approved methods (e.g., VPN with MFA)
- Avoid using personal devices for work without approval

---

## Restricted Activities

Users must NOT:

- Use company branding for personal or external purposes
- Use company email for non-work activities
- Store non-company data on company systems
- Perform cryptocurrency mining or unauthorized computations
- Access data without authorization
- Use someone else’s credentials
- Download unauthorized software or copyrighted content

---

## Security Risks

Users must not:

- Bypass security controls
- Create or distribute malware
- Represent the company without approval
- Use personal email for company business
- Upload company data to personal storage platforms
- Modify or delete critical data without authorization
- Install unauthorized software

---

## Exit Responsibilities

- Employees must return all company assets upon exit
- HR will manage asset recovery if needed

---

## Right to Monitor

- Users should not expect privacy in company systems
- YOTTABYTE may monitor usage of all IT resources
- Emails and work data are company property
- Authorized personnel may access accounts if required for security or compliance

---

## Annual Review

- This policy is reviewed annually or when required
- Internal audits are conducted yearly
- Processes are updated to ensure compliance with latest standards

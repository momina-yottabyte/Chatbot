# HRMS User Guide

## Leave Application

To apply for leave:

- Login to the HRMS system
- Navigate to the "Leave" section
- Click on "Apply Leave"
- Select leave type (Casual, Sick, Annual)
- Choose start and end date
- Submit the request for approvals   

## Attendance (Check-In / Check-Out)

To mark attendance:

- Login to your dashboard
- Click on "Check-In" when starting your workday
- Click on "Check-Out" when ending your workday

Notes:
- You can only check-in once per day
- Late check-in may be marked as late attendance

## Leave Policy

- Employees are entitled to:
  - 12 Casual Leaves per year
  - 10 Sick Leaves per year
- Leave requests must be approved by the manager
- Unapproved leaves may be marked as unpaid

# 1. PURPOSE

This handbook provides employees with essential information about company policies, procedures, expectations, and guidelines to ensure a professional, secure, and productive work environment.

---

# 2. SCOPE

This handbook applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties working with or on behalf of YOTTABYTE.

---

# 3. COMPANY OVERVIEW

YOTTABYTE is committed to maintaining high standards of professionalism, security, and compliance with Information Security Management System (ISMS) practices.

---

# 4. CODE OF CONDUCT

- Employees must act with integrity and professionalism.
- Respect colleagues, clients, and company property.
- Avoid conflicts of interest.
- Maintain confidentiality of company information.
- Follow all company policies and procedures.

---

# 5. EMPLOYMENT POLICIES

- Equal opportunity employment is practiced.
- No discrimination or harassment is tolerated.
- Employees must adhere to company rules and regulations.

---

# 6. ATTENDANCE & LEAVE

- Employees must maintain punctuality.
- Leaves must be approved by management.
- Unauthorized absence may lead to disciplinary action.

---

# 7. INFORMATION SECURITY RESPONSIBILITIES

- Employees must protect company data and systems.
- Follow all ISMS policies and procedures.
- Report any security incidents immediately.

---

# 8. ACCEPTABLE USE OF IT RESOURCES

- IT resources are for official use only.
- Unauthorized software installation is prohibited.
- Internet and email usage must comply with company policies.

---

# 9. DATA PROTECTION & CONFIDENTIALITY

- Sensitive data must not be shared without authorization.
- Follow data protection and privacy policies.
- Ensure secure handling of information.

---

# 10. DISCIPLINARY ACTIONS

Failure to comply with company policies may result in:

- Warning
- Suspension
- Termination

---

# 11. HEALTH & SAFETY

- Employees must follow safety guidelines.
- Report hazards or unsafe conditions immediately.
- Maintain a safe working environment.

---

# 12. ANNUAL REVIEW

This handbook shall be reviewed annually or whenever required to ensure compliance with company policies and regulatory standards.

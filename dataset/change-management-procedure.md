## 1. Change Management Procedure

### 1.1 Purpose

This procedure defines the methodology for handling change requests and managing the change management lifecycle.

---

### 1.2 Responsibility

The following roles are responsible:

- YOTTABYTE Management  
- Digital Operations Lead  
- Information Security Officer  

---

### 1.3 Change Management Lifecycle

The change management lifecycle defines the steps involved in handling and implementing changes within the organization.

---

### 1.4 Procedure Statements

1. Employees initiate a change request by filling out a **Change Request Form (CRF)** and submitting it to:
   - `secops@yottabyte.ltd`

2. The change initiator must:
   - Get the CRF signed by their manager or Change Manager  
   - Then email it to the SecOps group  

3. The **Digital Operations Lead** will:
   - Create a ticket in Jira  
   - Share the ticket ID with the change initiator  

4. Once the ticket is created:
   - The **change management lifecycle (Section 1.3)** is followed  

5. All changes will:
   - Be discussed in **ISF meetings**  
   - Include **risk assessment by the Information Security Officer (ISO)**  

---

## 2. Annual Review

- ISMS documents are reviewed annually or upon updates  
- Internal audits must be conducted annually  
- Reports are submitted to the Information Security Forum (ISF)

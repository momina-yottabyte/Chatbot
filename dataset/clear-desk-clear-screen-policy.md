## 1. Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to reduce the risk of unauthorized access, loss, and damage to information when workstations, laptops, or servers are left unattended.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all systems, people, and processes that constitute YOTTABYTE’s information systems, including employees and third parties.

---

## 3. Policy Statements

### 3.1 Clear Desk

- All employees must clear desks of sensitive or confidential documents at the end of each workday  
- Sensitive documents must be stored in secure cabinets or drawers  
- Unnecessary papers must be shredded or disposed of securely  
- Personal belongings should be stored in designated areas  
- Printed materials must be immediately collected from printers or fax machines  
- Printing should only be done when absolutely necessary  
- Visitors must always be escorted in work areas  
- Passwords must not be written down or stored physically  
- Keys and access cards must not be left unattended  

---

### 3.2 Clear Screen

- Employees are accountable for all activity performed using their accounts  
- Screens must be locked when leaving workstations  
- Password-protected screen savers activate after 5 minutes of inactivity  
- Passwords must be:
  - Complex  
  - Changed regularly  
  - Never shared  

- Screens must be positioned to prevent unauthorized viewing  
- Extra caution required when working in public places  
- Access to sensitive data must be restricted to authorized personnel only  

---

## 4. Annual Review

- ISMS documents are reviewed annually or upon updates  
- Internal audits must be conducted yearly  
- Reports are submitted to the Information Security Forum (ISF)
